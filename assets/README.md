# Photo Placeholder

Please add your professional photo to this directory with the filename: **Myphoto.png**

## Photo Requirements:

- **Filename:** Myphoto.png (case-sensitive)
- **Recommended size:** 280x280 pixels or larger (will be displayed at 280x280)
- **Format:** PNG (preferred) or JPG
- **Style:** Professional executive headshot
- **Background:** Neutral or solid color recommended
- **Attire:** Business formal

## Tips for Executive Photos:

- Use good lighting (natural light or professional setup)
- Neutral expression or slight professional smile
- Face the camera directly
- Ensure sharp focus
- Conservative, professional appearance
- Consider Saudi/GCC business culture standards

Once you add your photo, the website will automatically display it in the hero section.
